A family night like no other

What happens at the Wiener Nationals

It is part race night, part block party. Bring the whole family for a night that goes well beyond the green flag.

The Wienermobile

The one and only Oscar Mayer Wienermobile rolls onto the clay as the official pace car. A photo-op the kids will not stop talking about.

Hot dog eating contest

A crowd favorite. Step up and compete, or cheer on the brave souls who do, before the racing heats up.

Live entertainment

Special guests and live entertainment between the action, with plenty to keep the night moving.

Fireworks

A full fireworks show caps off the night once the checkered flag flies. A perfect end to a family evening at the track.

Late Models & B-Mods

Real racing on the card: Late Models and B-Mods battling on the 3/8-mile clay oval under the lights.

For a good cause

Proceeds benefit Missouri State Highway Patrol charities, so a night at the track gives back to the troopers who serve our roads.

Racing for the troopers

Proceeds benefit MSHP charities

Every Wiener Nationals supports Missouri State Highway Patrol charities, including the Missouri State Highway Patrol Foundation and the Missouri Association of State Troopers Emergency Relief Society (MASTERS). When you buy a ticket or sponsor the night, you are helping the families and causes those organizations stand behind.
