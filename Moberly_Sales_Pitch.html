In plain terms

Three things quietly costing you ticket sales

01

Your best pages are switched off

Points, schedule, ticket prices, rules, and driver forms are all set to hide from search. When someone Googles "Moberly races this weekend," the pages that would answer them never appear.

Lost: high-intent fans ready to come
02

Your info is trapped in pictures

The schedule, prices, and rules are flat images. Google can't read them, and on a phone they shrink to an unreadable thumbnail. Most race fans are on a phone, often deciding that afternoon.

Lost: the mobile gameday decision
03

Mixed signals on contact

Two different phone numbers show on every page, and every update points fans to Facebook. Search engines and first-time visitors both read inconsistency as "is this place even still open?"

Lost: trust and local ranking
